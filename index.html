My Home Coffee Bar

My collection of coffee makers includes

  • the Breville Grind Control automatic drip coffee maker,
  • the Hario V60-02 Red Coffee Dripper pourover coffee maker,
  • the Primula Classic 4 Cup Coffee Press french press, and
  • the Gaggia Classic semi-automatic espresso machine.

To enable brewing freshly ground coffee I use the Breville Smart Grinder Pro, a conical burr grinder. The Grind Control also featues a built-in flat burr grinder.
